Garlic Lime Chicken Fajitas

  • Garlic
  • Minced Onions
  • Cumin
  • Oregano
  • Cilantro
  • 1/4 Cup Lime Juice
  • 1/4 Cup Orange Juice
  • 2 Tbs Olive Oil
  • Salt & pepper, to taste
  • 1 1/2 pounds Chicken Breasts (boneless, skinless), cut into strips
  • 1 Green or Red Pepper, medium
  • 1 Onion, medium
  • 8 Flour Tortillas
  1. Mix: juices, oil and spices. Set aside 1/4 cup of marinade.
  2. Place chicken and marinade in a bag or glass container and let refridgerate minimum of 30 minutes.
  3. Cook and stir chicken in large heated skilled (med-high heat) for 3 minutes or until chicken is lightly browned, remove chicken from skillet.
  4. Cook pepper, onion and marinade that was set aside for 5 minutes, until tender.
  5. Add chicken back and cook additional 2-3 minutes.
  6. Warm tortillas in oven or microwave briefly and serve!

Best served with sour cream, salsa, guacamole or hot sauce!
